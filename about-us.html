About Us

Equipment Souk has been launched as a marketplace with an intention of being a transparent bridge between sellers and buyers of preloved commercial kitchen equipment, commercial refrigeration, commercial freezers and supermarket equipment. One of the fastest-growing online marketplaces for buying and selling used and refurbished. Our platform is fully dedicated to providing local customers with top-notch items and services and generating best value and growth opportunities for the vendors.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to become the most valuable and diverse online marketplace - the most trustworthy bridge between our vendors and customers. We measurably contribute to the profitability and growth of our customers and resellers or vendors.

  • We believe in delivering value for customers and provide solutions to their problems in an effective, convenient, fast, and simple way. We do our utmost to ensure all products uploaded on our marketplace are genuine.

  • For resellers and vendors, we aim at creating a platform through profit and sales opportunities and demand generation while giving broad access to several markets and customers and innovative offerings.

OUR VALUES AND PRINCIPLES

Our diverse marketplace runs on some core principles and values. Our extensively driven team share a fundamental mind-set that evolves to keep up with the expectations of our precious customers and notable vendors and the forever-changing social conditions.
Our core principle is to make our online marketplace the safest and trustable platform where everyone can buy and sell with utmost comfort and convenience. We always believe in being totally transparent about the pricing and transaction process. So any hidden pricing or falsified details of products do not appear on our platform.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Equipment Souk intends to have the best collection of preloved commercial kitchen equipment, commercial refrigeration, commercial freezer equipment, and supermarket equipment.

We offer outstanding sales and profit opportunities with the much-needed freedom to the vendors and resellers.

We work hard to make the experience of every customer with us seamless, pleasurable, and peaceful.

We focus on increasing our services to exceed the expectations of our customers and find innovative ways to enhance the growth of our associates and suppliers
